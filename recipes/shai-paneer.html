Shahi Paneer

What is Shahi Paneer?

Shahi Paneer can literally be translated to “royal paneer.” My recipe is that of a rich, creamy curry made with a base of nuts, onions, yogurt and spices. Paneer is a fresh cheese that is popular in Indian cuisine. It is made by curdling milk with a food acid (often lemon juice), then pressed into cubes. The most notable thing about paneer cheese is that it does not melt, providing a texture similar to tofu.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cashews, soaked in water
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp ghee
  • 1/2 tsp oil

Instructions/ Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger garlic paste. Saute for a minute.
  2. Add onions and saute till they turn translucent.
  3. Add tomatoes and saute till they turn mushy.
  4. Add cashews and saute for a minute.
  5. Add yogurt and saute till the oil separates.
  6. Add water and bring to a boil.
  7. Add all the spices and mix well.
  8. Add paneer and mix well.
  9. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  10. Turn off the heat and serve hot with roti or naan.
