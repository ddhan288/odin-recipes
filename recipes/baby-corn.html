Baby Corn Chilly

what is Baby Corn Chilly

Baby corn chilly is a popular Indo-Chinese dish. It is a simple and easy to make recipe. Baby corn is a popular vegetable in the Indo-Chinese cuisine. It is a popular starter in Chinese restaurants. Baby corn is a popular vegetable in the Indo-Chinese cuisine. It is a popular starter in Chinese restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup baby corn
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup red chilli sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup corn flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup red chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add onions and saute till they turn golden brown.
  2. Add capsicum and saute till they turn soft.
  3. Add baby corn and saute for 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and saute till they turn mushy.
  5. Add red chilli sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, corn flour, water, red chilli powder and salt and mix well.
  6. Baby Corn Chilly is ready to serve.
