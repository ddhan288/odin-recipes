What is Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is one of Indiaâ€™s most popular paneer gravy recipes, and with good reason! Indian cottage cheese cubes are smothered in a creamy, lightly spiced tomato sauce that is downright delicious. With my video and step-by-step guide you can easily make this restaurant style Paneer Butter Masala recipe at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup kasuri methi, crushed
  • 1/2 cup garam masala
  • 1/2 cup red chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup kasuri methi, crushed
  • 1/2 cup garam masala
  • 1/2 cup red chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup cumin powder

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add onions and saute till they turn golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes and saute till they turn mushy.
  3. Add all the dry spices and mix well.
  4. Add cream and milk and mix well.
  5. Add water and mix well.
  6. Add paneer and mix well.
  7. Add kasuri methi and mix well.
  8. Add coriander leaves and mix well.
  9. Paneer Butter Masala is ready to serve.
